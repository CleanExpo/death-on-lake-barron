# Death on Lake Barron - Murder Mystery Game

A forensic murder mystery game built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Investigate the death of Tyler James Bradshaw, examine 15 pieces of evidence, interview 4 suspects, and solve the crime.

## How to Play

1. **Start Investigation** - Choose your difficulty level
2. 2. **Review Evidence** - Examine 15 pieces of forensic evidence
   3. 3. **Interview Suspects** - Learn about the key players
      4. 4. **Follow the Timeline** - Understand the sequence of events
         5. 5. **Make Your Deduction** - Answer 3 critical questions
            6. 6. **Submit Your Verdict** - See if you solved the case correctly
